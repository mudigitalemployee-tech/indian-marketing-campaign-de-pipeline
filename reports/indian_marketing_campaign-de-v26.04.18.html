Data Architecture Design

This section explains how the pipeline is structured : think of it as a blueprint. Data flows through three layers: Bronze (raw, untouched data), Silver (cleaned and organized data), and Gold (business-ready summaries). This layered approach ensures that clean, reliable data is always available for reporting and analysis.

Architecture Pattern

The architecture pattern is automatically selected based on your data characteristics : volume, source type, column types, PII presence, duplicates : and the business purpose you described. It is not a fixed default. This ensures the design fits your specific use case.

Selected Pattern MEDALLION   HIGH CONFIDENCE
What It MeansBronze (raw) → Silver (cleaned/conformed) → Gold (aggregated/business-ready)
Best Used ForAnalytics, BI dashboards, reporting, large datasets with complex transformations

Why this pattern was selected:

  • Strong analytics/BI intent detected (6 keywords matched) → Medallion pattern
  • Small dataset (1,025 rows) → Simple ETL may be sufficient
  • 6 numeric columns → aggregation in Gold layer adds value
  • PII detected in 2 column(s) → Silver masking layer required
  • Null values present → Silver cleaning layer recommended
